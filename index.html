Bonnell 6" Full Mattress | M96321

This budget-friendly firm full mattress offers a comfortable night’s sleep at a comfortably affordable price. At the core of this mattress: 13.5-gauge tempered Bonnell steel coils that provide maximum support for a sound sleep. The mattress’s quality foam and quilted polyester fabric add an enhanced level of comfort. Foundation available, sold separately.
